ABOUT US

DC Cosplay Creatures is a creative costume and design shop
based in Antipolo, specializing in cosplay costumes, gowns, and
custom outfit creations to life through detailed craftsmanship,
creativity, and passion for wearable art.

From fantasy and gaming inspired costumes to special event
outfits and themed designs, we work hard to create pieces that
help our clients stand out with confidence. Every project is
made with care, originality, and attention to detail.
